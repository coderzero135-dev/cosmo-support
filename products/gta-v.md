# GTA V Cheats

## PC Cheat Codes

Press `~` to open the console, then type the code.

### Player Cheats
```
PAINKILLER    - Invincibility (5 min)
HOTHANDS      - Explosive melee attacks
TURTLE        - Full health & armor
POWERUP       - Recharge special ability
SKYDIVE       - Get a parachute
```

### Weapons & Ammo

HIGHEXPLOSIVE - Explosive bullets
