## Glitches & Exploits

### Infinite Rupees
1. Go to Hebra region
2. Hunt animals → cook 5 Gourmet Meats
3. Sell to Beedle for 490 rupees each

### Weapon Duplication
1. Equip weapon + shield
2. Pause → drop weapon → unpause → quick menu
3. Pick up weapon → duplicate spawns

### Durability Transfer
1. Overload menu in Kakariko Village
2. Swap durability from high-durability item
3. Keep any weapon forever

### Whistle Sprint
Hold whistle (D-pad down) and mash sprint — run without using stamina.
